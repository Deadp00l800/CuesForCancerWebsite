Years of Impact
Empowering Lives Through Every Cue

Community, creativity, and connection

Cues for Cancer is a Norfolk, Virginia–based nonprofit creating meaningful impact through the power of community, creativity, and connection — supporting those affected by cancer through engaging events, performances, and collaborative experiences.

Community Support Events

Gatherings that bring survivors, families, and neighbors together to feel seen, supported, and less alone.

Creative Spark Workshops

Theatre, music, and art-based workshops that give participants a creative outlet through diagnosis and recovery.

Fundraising Initiatives

Performances and campaigns that fund direct support for families navigating the cost of a cancer journey.

Volunteer Opportunities

Ways for performers, professionals, and neighbors to lend their time and talent to our mission.
